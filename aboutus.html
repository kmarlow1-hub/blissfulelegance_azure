About Blissful Elegance Co.

Our Story

Blissful Elegance Co. is more than just a clothing brand — it’s a celebration of simplicity, quality, and conscious living. Founded in 1978, our founder, Bliss Chofield, always believed that true elegance comes from timeless designs that elevate everyday life with minimal effort. She made her way to the top after working with other popular designers such as Gianni Versace and Karl Diane von Furstenberg. Our collections are always crafted with care, using premium materials to ensure long-lasting quality and a perfect fit.

Our Values

Eco-friendly

At Blissful Elegance Co., we prioritize the planet by sourcing materials responsibly and using eco-friendly practices to minimize our impact on the environment.

Community

Together, we can create a lasting impact — where elegance meets responsibility, and fashion fosters connection. We work together with local organizations to donate clothes to those in need.
